Automating Legal Text Summarization with Multi-Agent Systems and Human-Centered Evaluation

My MS research project focused on Automating Legal Text Summarization with Multi-Agent Systems and Human-Centered Evaluation. The project aimed to streamline the process of summarizing complex legal documents using advanced AI techniques. I developed a multi-agent system where each agent specialized in different tasks, such as extracting key points, rephrasing content, and evaluating the summaries. The system utilized machine learning and natural language processing (NLP) models to generate concise and accurate summaries while maintaining the essence of legal documents. To ensure the quality and reliability of the system, I integrated a human-centered evaluation framework, which included custom evaluation metrics like ROUGE scores to assess the summaries' effectiveness compared to human-generated summaries. This project demonstrated the potential of AI in improving the efficiency of legal processes, providing valuable insights into the application of multi-agent systems in the legal domain.
